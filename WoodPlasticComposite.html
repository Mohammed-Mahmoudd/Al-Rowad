Wood Plastic Composite Solutions

Our Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) products represent Al Rowad's commitment to sustainable construction materials, offering durable and eco-friendly alternatives to traditional wood and plastic. We combine innovation with environmental consciousness to create superior products that transform living and working spaces.

Our WPC solutions support Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 sustainability goals through green manufacturing processes, reduced reliance on natural resources, and long-lasting, recyclable building materials.

  • Durable composite materials resistant to weathering and decay
  • Eco-friendly alternative to traditional wood products
  • Customizable designs for various applications
  • Low maintenance and long-lasting performance
